## Content Pillars (40/30/20/10 Mix)

### 1. Educational (40%)
- Loan eligibility explainers (Sheikh Zayed Housing Programme)
- Contract red flags and what to watch for
- Step-by-step guides (loan application, contractor selection, handover)
- "Know your numbers" posts (AED amounts, timelines, coverage)
- Building permit and approval process walkthroughs

### 2. Engagement (30%)
- Story polls: "Did you know?" facts about construction contracts
- Q&A sessions: "Ask BCX" weekly
- "This or That" comparisons (fixed price vs cost-plus contracts)
- Community questions about housing loan experiences
- Before/After transformations of reviewed contracts

### 3. Promotional (20%)
- Free consultation CTA posts
- Client success stories (anonymized)
- "We saved AED X for this client" case studies
- WhatsApp CTA with specific offer
- Service spotlight (one service per week)

### 4. Cultural/Inspirational (10%)
- UAE National Day content
- Ramadan greetings and relevant content
- Emirati homeowner success celebrations
- Arabic wisdom quotes about building/home

## Scheduling Workflow

### Tools (all free)
1. **Canva Business** — design posts, carousels, Reels covers
2. **Meta Business Suite** — schedule and auto-publish to Instagram
3. **Google Sheets** — content calendar tracking

### Weekly Routine (~2 hours/week)
1. **Saturday 30 min:** Plan next week's 5 posts in Google Sheets (topic, format, hashtag set)
2. **Saturday 60 min:** Design all 5 posts in Canva (use templates)
3. **Saturday 30 min:** Schedule all 5 in Meta Business Suite with captions + hashtags
4. **Daily 5 min:** Check comments, reply to DMs, respond to Story interactions

## Instagram Bio Link Page

The `/links` page on bcx.ae serves as the bio link destination. It includes:
- Book a Free Consultation → /contact (tracked via UTM)
- WhatsApp Us → WhatsApp with tracking
- Our Services → /HOWWEPROTECTYOU (tracked via UTM)
- About BCX → /about (tracked via UTM)

All clicks are tracked in GA4 with `linkinbio_click` events.

### UTM Structure
Every link from Instagram includes:
- `utm_source=instagram`
- `utm_medium=linkinbio` (bio link) or `utm_medium=social` (post links)
- `utm_campaign=links_[destination]`

View in GA4: Reports → Acquisition → Traffic acquisition → Filter by source "instagram"
