# Emirati Construction Expert

Welcome to the **Emirati Construction Expert** (formerly Contract Management Expert), a comprehensive platform tailored specifically for Emiratis to manage their construction projects effectively. Whether you are building your dream home, a majlis, or an investment property in the UAE, this tool is designed to simplify the complexities of local construction management and protect your interests.

## 🏗️ Why This Matters

Building a home or property in the UAE involves navigating a unique landscape of local regulations, contractor negotiations, and financial planning. Many Emiratis face challenges such as:
- Misunderstandings in contract terms, scope, and BOQ (Bill of Quantities).
- Delays in project delivery and missed milestones.
- Unforeseen costs, hidden fees, and budget overruns.
- Navigating local municipality approvals (e.g., Dubai Municipality, Abu Dhabi DMT, Sharjah Municipality) and utility connections (DEWA, ADDC, SEWA).

This platform acts as your digital advisor, ensuring your rights are protected and your project stays on track.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Smart Contract Management**: Templates and review tools aligned with UAE local laws, designed to protect the owner from unfair clauses.
- **Contractor & Consultant Vetting**: Criteria and checklists to evaluate, interview, and select the most reliable local contractors and engineering consultants.
- **Budget & Payment Tracking**: Monitor payments strictly linked to project milestones and engineer approvals, ensuring you only pay for completed work.
- **Milestone & Progress Monitoring**: Keep track of construction phases from design and permitting to foundation, superstructure, finishing, and final handover.
- **Dispute Avoidance**: Clear documentation and communication trails to prevent common disputes between owners and contractors.

## 🚀 Getting Started

To run the application locally:

1. Install dependencies:
   ```bash
   npm install
   ```

2. Start the development server:
   ```bash
   npm run dev
   ```

3. Open your browser and navigate to the local host address provided in the terminal.
